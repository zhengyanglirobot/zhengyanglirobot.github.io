AI-Enabled Cooperative Surgical Robots

Cooperative surgical robots represent an emerging paradigm in robotic-assisted surgery, offering superior flexibility and accessibility unmatched by conventional fixed-base systems. Unlike bulky, OR-confined traditional robots, they are compact and mobile, enabling deployment across operating rooms, clinics, and field hospitals. They can autonomously perform routine low-risk tasks such as instrument handover, camera repositioning, tissue retraction, and port-site suturing after minimally invasive surgery, easing surgeons’ workload and improving clinical outcomes. This research develops the first AI-powered cooperative surgical robot, integrating visual-language reasoning, high-fidelity world modeling, sim-to-real learning, and safety-assured control to expand robotic assistance in diverse healthcare settings.
